Project aims:

  • To develop a Mobile Web App for playing treasure hunts.
  • Access a variety of different treasure hunts based on different type of question like multiple choice, true/false, numeric etc.
  • Some questions may require you to be at specific locations or even scan a QR code to find hints for the answer.
  • Moreover, we performed some testing to make sure the functionality of our app and that is well accepted by users. Press the link to go to our testing page: Testing Page
